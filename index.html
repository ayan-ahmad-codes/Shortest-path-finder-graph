🎯 How It Works

Dijkstra's Algorithm

We use Dijkstra's shortest path algorithm to find the optimal route between your pickup and drop-off locations, ensuring minimum distance and time.

Graph-Based Navigation

City locations are represented as nodes in a graph, with roads as weighted edges. The algorithm efficiently computes the shortest path.

Dynamic Pricing

Fare is calculated based on distance, estimated time, vehicle type, and time-of-day surge pricing to give you an accurate estimate.
